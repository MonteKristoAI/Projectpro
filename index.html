FAQ

Common Questions
Answered

What types of projects are included?
ProjectPro focuses on commercial construction — multifamily residential, hospitality (hotels, resorts), office, retail, mixed-use, industrial, and institutional projects. These are projects where building material specifications are made by architects and developers — the exact decision-makers we include contact information for.
How is the contact information verified?
Our research team manually verifies every contact before it's included in your list. We confirm names, titles, direct phone numbers, and email addresses on a rolling basis. If a contact is outdated or unverifiable, it doesn't make it into your list. This is why subscribers see industry-leading email open rates — you're reaching real people, not generic office inboxes.
What stage are most projects in?
Approximately 95% of projects on your list are in the planning or pre-construction phase — typically 3 to 6 months before the bid stage. This is exactly when architectural specifications are being written, making it the ideal time for building material suppliers to engage.
Do projects repeat on the list week to week?
Projects only appear on your list if they are new, or still actively in a pre-bid stage with new information. Once a project moves past the bid stage and into construction, it's removed. This keeps your team's time focused on projects where engagement can still make a difference.
Can I add more users to my subscription?
Yes — all subscriptions include unlimited users at no additional cost. You can forward the weekly Excel list to your entire sales team, regional managers, or executives. There are no per-seat fees. Your cost is the same whether one person uses it or twenty.
